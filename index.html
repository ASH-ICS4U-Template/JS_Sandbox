Before You Book Your Event, Read This!

  • 1. Click "Book Event": You'll see the Introduction to the Booking Page. Click the big box that says "ASH Events Booking Form" to move on.
  • 2. Pick Your Date and Time: Choose when you want your event to start. A new form will pop up for you to fill in all the details. Don't worry, we'll confirm everything, including the end time, since Google Calendar doesn't let you set one.
  • 3. Share the Details: All info goes to me, my Tek team, Kristine, Angela, and Bryan. Be as detailed as possible. If you need to add more, go ahead!
  • 4. More Details = Better Events: The more info you give, the easier it is for us and the custodians to set up your amazing event.
  • 5. Not Applicable? If something doesn't apply to you, just put "NA."
  • 6. Book Early: Try to book as early as possible, especially for after-school events.
